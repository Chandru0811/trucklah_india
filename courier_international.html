Air freight

Air freight is the ideal choice for high-value, time-sensitive, or perishable shipments. We have world-class air cargo services to meet your demands. Send your gifts worldwide, celebrating life's special moments together.

Sea freight

If you are planning global bulk shipping, sea freight provides a reliable and cost-effective way to transport your goods. Sea freight connects continents, and you can do bulk shipping effectively across the globe.

Road freight

Road freight is often the fastest and most efficient mode of transport for shorter distances. Road networks provide efficient door-to-door delivery, even to remote locations, also cost-effective for smaller shipments.

Rail freight

Best suitable for bulk shipments for long distances. Reliable schedules, comparatively reduced accidents, reduced road congestions, lower operating costs, and fuel efficiency make rail freight most demanding and attractive.

Express or Courier Freight

Express or Courier Freight become indispensable in today's fast-paced global economy. Real-time tracking adds more reliability to this method. Express delivery and reduced transit time make this freight more popular.

Our Work Process

 Book order

Place an order, either online, over the app on the phone or call, or in person. Get the quotation for your order. Fill in the necessary details including quantity, date, time, shipping address, and payment information.

 Confirm order

Once you are satisfied with the quote, confirm the quantity, date, time, shipping address, and payment information. Once the payment is made, the order will be processed. You can also opt for packing assistance.

 Rider assigned

Once your payment is made and the order is confirmed, the rider will be assigned to you. The contact number of the rider will be provided with the vehicle number.

 Order pickup

You can schedule the date, time, and location of the pickup. You can directly report to the rider if you have any queries or changes in location or time. You will be given packing assistance if you desire.

 Air freight

Your air freight will be expertly handled, including customs and paperwork. With our extensive reach across 220 countries and territories, we're committed to delivering your shipments worldwide, on time and intact.

 Tracking order

You can track your order live on your mobile phone till it reaches the destination. Your precious belongings will be handled with utmost care and delivered punctually, adhering to the specified schedule.
