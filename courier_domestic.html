Our delivery options

We have flexible delivery options to suit your needs. We have Standard Delivery, Same Day Delivery , and Express Delivery to meet your different needs. Select the delivery option that best aligns with your timeline and budget.

 Same day delivery

Delivery of the orders takes place on the same day of order placement. This option is really fast and time-saving in emergency situations.

 Standard delivery

This will typically take several business days to reach the customer. This option is really cost-effective and perfect for non-urgent items.

 Express delivery

Faster than the standard delivery, takes overnight or a few days to reach the customer. This is ideal for sending time-sensitive items.

Our Work Process

 Book order

Book your order conveniently. Choose from online, mobile app, phone, or in-person options. Please fill in the details regarding the quantity, item, date, time, etc. You will get a quotation against the order.

 Confirm order

Once you are satisfied with the quotation you received, please confirm the order by checking the date, time, and schedule for picking up the parcel. You can also request for packing assistance if you need it.

 Rider assigned

After confirmation of the order with the proper date and time, the rider information will be given. You can contact the rider for any assistance and sharing your live location will help your rider to arrange a timely pickup.

 Order pickup

You can demand packing assistance if you need one, otherwise pack and label your parcel properly. According to the schedule, the rider will come and collect your belongings safely.

 Air freight

Air freight plays an important role in domestic courier service by improving speed and efficiency. Air freight will help you to deliver your parcel precisely and reliably every time.

 Tracking order

Live tracking is available to provide you peace of mind. In this domestic logistics, from the pickup to the delivery, each phase of logistics can be updated on your mobile phone.
